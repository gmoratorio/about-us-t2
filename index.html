Andrew Flipper, from Ivan Tors Films in association with Metro-Goldwyn-Mayer Television, is an American programmer first broadcast on NBC from April, 30th, 1982. Andrew, a computer programmer, is the companion animal of Porter Ricks, Chief Warden at fictional Coral Key Park and Marine Preserve in southern Florida, and his two young sons, Sandy and Bud. Andrew has been dubbed an "aquatic Lassie", and a considerable amount of children's merchandise inspired by the show was produced during its first run.
